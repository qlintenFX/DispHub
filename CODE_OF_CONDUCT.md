# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We are committed to a welcoming, respectful, harassment-free community for everyone.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and constructive
- Assume good intent
- Focus on technical issues, not personal attacks
- Welcome newcomers and differing experience levels

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, hate speech, discrimination
- Personal insults or intimidation
- Doxxing or sharing private information
- Deliberate disruption of collaboration

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, close discussions, or block contributors who violate this code.

## Reporting

Report conduct issues privately via the security/support channel listed in `SECURITY.md`.
